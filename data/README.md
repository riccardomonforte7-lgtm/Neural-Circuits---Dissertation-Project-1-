## Data

This project integrates two main data sources: a transcriptomic MERFISH dataset and a single-neuron reconstruction/projectomic dataset.

### MERFISH dataset

The MERFISH dataset contains spatial transcriptomic information for neurons located in the entorhinal cortex of the mouse brain. The data were obtained from the Allen Brain Cell Atlas:

https://alleninstitute.github.io/abc_atlas_access/descriptions/MERFISH-C57BL6J-638850-imputed.html

For further details, see the original publication:

Zhang, M., Pan, X., Jung, W., et al. (2023). *Molecularly defined and spatially resolved cell atlas of the whole mouse brain*. Nature, 624, 343–354.  
https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06812-z

### Single-neuron reconstruction / projectomic dataset

The single-neuron reconstruction dataset contains axonal projection information for individual neurons and was used to study connectivity patterns and their relationship with soma position.

For further details, see:

Qiu, S., et al. (2024). *Whole-brain spatial organization of hippocampal single-neuron projectomes*. Science, 383(6682).  
https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.adj9198

### Data availability

The raw datasets are not included in this repository. In particular, some data are subject to data-use agreements and cannot be redistributed publicly.

Users interested in accessing the original data should refer to the links above for official documentation, access instructions, and usage conditions.
